Parking

Parking at the church is restricted but there is plenty of nearby parking within a short distance. The nearest car park is Water Meadow Car Park, HP5 1LF a short 5 min walk from the church and Staryard Car Park, HP5 1EP which is 8 mins walk to the church. Parking charges apply but are relatively cheap, and can be seen in advance here or here

After the ceremony we will head to ‘La Prateria’ for our reception. This is a private residence, with no parking available. However, there are many residential streets surrounding the venue which offer some parking as well as Chesham Leisure Centre, White Hill, HP5 1BB which is a short walk away. It might be easiest to leave your car in one of the suggested car parks in Chesham and use a taxi company to travel between sites, it’s a short distance and won’t cost much.

It is perfectly fine to do drop offs at either venue for guests who have mobility concerns, please let us know in advance of the day if you want any extra assistance.

Alternative-Travel

Ever wondered what lies at the end of the Metropolitan Line? Now’s your chance to find out! Trains travel directly from Baker Street to Chesham and from the station there’s just a short walk to the church or ‘La Prateria’ (please check for rail replacement works as this will add to your journey time). It’s also possible to use Chiltern Rail from Marylebone to Amersham and then Chesham is a short taxi ride away. There are also trains to nearby Berkhamsted from London Euston and again Chesham is a short taxi ride away.

La Prateria

We are extremely grateful to our friends Simon and Marianne for the loan of their wonderful field. Our reception will take place on the field in a Sperry tent supplying ample cover should the weather be typically ‘British’.

Footwear

We recommend you check the weather before planning your footwear. Much of the party will be on grass, so may not be friendly for high heels or stilettos. If the weather turns and wellies are required, you don’t need to stand on ceremony and ruin good shoes, we certainly will be donning some waterproof footwear!
